# Besiktningsprotokoll — mallar

Open templates for Swedish construction inspection protocols. Use for slutbesiktning, garantibesiktning, särskild besiktning, efterbesiktning, and statusbesiktning.

Maintained by [Zaragoza AB](https://zaragoza.se), Helsingborg.

## Templates included

| File | Type | When used |
|---|---|---|
| [templates/slutbesiktning-abs18.md](templates/slutbesiktning-abs18.md) | Slutbesiktning (final inspection) | Consumer contract per ABS 18 |
| [templates/slutbesiktning-ab04.md](templates/slutbesiktning-ab04.md) | Slutbesiktning | Commercial contract per AB 04 |
| [templates/garantibesiktning.md](templates/garantibesiktning.md) | Garantibesiktning (2-year warranty inspection) | Before warranty expires |
| [templates/efterbesiktning.md](templates/efterbesiktning.md) | Efterbesiktning | Re-check of noted defects |
| [templates/statusbesiktning.md](templates/statusbesiktning.md) | Statusbesiktning | Pre-purchase / general condition |
| [templates/overlamningsprotokoll.md](templates/overlamningsprotokoll.md) | Handover protocol | Consumer handover, minor projects |

## Legal basis

- **AB 04 chapter 7** — slutbesiktning for commercial projects
- **ABS 18 points 14–18** — slutbesiktning for consumer projects
- **Konsumenttjänstlagen 12, 51 §§** — consumer rights at handover
- **Jordabalken 4 kap.** — statusbesiktning in property transactions
- **SBR / Byggingenjörerna** — certification of besiktningsmän

## How to use

1. Pick the template matching your contract type (ABS 18 for consumer, AB 04 for commercial)
2. Replace all `[bracketed]` placeholders
3. Walk through the property with contracting parties present
4. Fill the defect table (fel / anmärkning / observation / godkänt)
5. Both parties sign. Keep copies with timestamps.

## Important

These templates are starting points. For high-value projects or disputes, use a certified besiktningsman (SBR, Byggingenjörerna, RISE). Zaragoza AB can recommend certified inspectors in Skåne.
